The challenge

Back in 2019 the were only three designers and the old website was split between different outsourced platforms. It was hard to maintain the delivery process, collect metrics and run AB-testing. So we started to build our own in-house platform and a design system to achieve desired business results. As a design team lead I established a proper design process including research and discovery. This approach helped the team get fast and impressive results across all business metrics and dramatically reduce delivery time and the amount of UX-defects.

My role

I started as a Senior Product Designer in one of the teams and my primary goal was to adjust the design process and perform a complete redesign of the website. Soon after our first results I become a Design Team Lead responsible for the whole team including the Platform team.

üîç Step 1: Research

Analytics review

The first step was to collect a proper metrics. Most of the pages did not have any so we started by remapping them with a new metric tools.

User testing

The other crucial part in understanding the user behaviour is a proper user testing. With the help of our UX-research team we performed a number of offline sessions with users and collected the range of problems across all main products and pages.

Stakeholder interviews

Redesign would affect every business line in the company so I conducted a series of interviews with Product and Project Managers, Marketing team, Sales, Developers and Support representatives to understand their goals and needs.

Setting up our key metrics

After the research we set our metrics to measure the success of the redesign. The four major metrics are Convertions & Sales, Traffic & Search visibility, User Feedback & VOC, Delivery time.

Interesting fact

The hardest part of the main page was the main slider. It contains up to 6 slides and the numbers said that the first slide was the most clickable (96%) and all other slides got 4% combined. We tested more than 10 different variant before finding the right one with tabs and the preview of each slide. This variant divided total clicks between all of the slides and increased CR of each featured product in the main slider.

AB-testing & User testing

Each block and template was tested to check the CR. I used heatmaps, screen recordings and even the eye tracking to validate the best visual and storytelling.
